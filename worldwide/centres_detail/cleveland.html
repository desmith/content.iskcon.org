Brief details of centre
ISKCON Cleveland is a large and diverse nama hatta community without a formal temple. Classes and programs vary by location, with the exception of our weekly bhakti vrksa program, held every week at Prabhupada Manor in Fairview Park, OH. All programs are open to the public.

ISKCON Cleveland members and supporters also organize a yearly Festival of India and Ratha Yatra, Food for Life prgrams, and sankirtan preaching. Please contact us to learn more. We look forward to hearing from you. There is currently no active website for this community.
