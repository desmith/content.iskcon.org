Manchester  
Type of centre: Temple

Contact details
Address: 20 Mayfield Road
Whalley Range
Manchester, M16 8FT
UK

Ph: +44-(0)161-2264416
E-mail: manchester@iskcon.org.uk
Website: www.iskcon.org.uk/manchester

Activities

We go out regularly on harinam (public chanting) and book distribution, spreading Krishna's glories through singing, and the books of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada.

Programmes are held every Sunday from 2–5 pm, and consist of group chanting (kirtan), dancing and discussion on the Bhagavad-gita. At the end, there is a vegetarian feast.

Directions

Mayfield Road runs between Alexandra Road and Withington Road. A prominent landmark on the corner of Mayfield Road is a Netto supermarket. Bus number 16 (and others) runs from Manchester city centre into Whalley Range. We are about two miles south of the city centre.
