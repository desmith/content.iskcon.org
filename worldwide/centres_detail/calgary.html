Facilities

Gift Shop and Book Store
 This is the best place in Calgary to acquire authorised books on the ancient science of Krishna Consciousness.

Reasonably priced, these books are packed with knowledge of karma, reincarnation, transmigration of the soul, life's ultimate goal etc.

You can also acquire other items such as videos, devotional paraphernalia, and exotic clothing.

Call 403-265-3302 for further information

Hare Krishna Food For Life
The Calgary temple does a regular monthly Food For Life programme. You can also take part.

For further information call Baladev Dasa at 403-285-3463.

Other Information:

The Calgary temple offers the following services.
Nam Karan (Name Giving ceremony)
Anna Prashan (first Grains ceremony)
Mundan (Hair Cutting ceremony)
Vivaha (Marriage ceremony)
Griha Pravesha (Entering New House)
Antyesti Kriya (Funeral Rites)

For further information, please contact Radhavind Dasa 403-265-3302.
