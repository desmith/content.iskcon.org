Sunday Feast
The Sunday Feast is held at our Food For Life Restaurant. It starts 5pm every Sunday at: Food For Life, 200 William Street, Northbridge. Just before the Horseshoe Bridge going towards the City Centre.

The Sunday Feast has it all: singing, dancing, philosophy and a great feast. All this for just a donation of $4 to cover food costs.

Food for Life Restaurant
Sometimes called the kitchen religion, the Hare Krishnas, are famous for quality wholesome cruelty-free food. This food, offered to God, is called prasadam (mercy). The Perth Food for Life team cook sumptous lunchtime meals Monday to Friday. They also provide budget meals for low income earners.

All You Can Eat. Open lunchtimes Monday to Friday 12 pm to 2.30 pm. $5.95 ($4.95 Concession) and Saturday 11:30 am to 2:30 pm.
Budget Meals Monday to Friday 5 pm to 6 pm ( Take-away only).
