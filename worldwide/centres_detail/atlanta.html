Brief details of centre

Established in October 1973. We are a community of Vaisnavas (devotees of Krishna) based in the Greater Atlanta Area.

We are the oldest Krishna temple in the Southeast of the United States. For the past 26 years we have been serving the community in many ways.

Deities: Sri Sri Radha Madana-Mohana, Sri Sri Jagannatha, Baladeva & Subhadra, Sri Sri Nitai Gaura.

 

Krishna Kids Corner
Established in 1981, the gurukula on Bloomsburg Avenue was to be a temporary stopover. Plans to build a school for 70 children at an asrama in Port Royal, Pa. fell through, and the group, originally relocating from upstate New York, had to split up. One came to Catonsville, where a Krishna temple had been established in 1975. Today, the gurukula has 13 students.
