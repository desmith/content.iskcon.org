Brief details of centre

Located in a residential part of the city, the temple is surrounded by beautiful gardens, which provide an extremely tranquil and peaceful atmosphere. There are asrama facilities for men and women to stay overnight.

The temple is easily reached by car and public transport.

In addition to regular Sunday programs, there is a bhakti-vrksa program on Friday evenings and the pandava-sena youth group host regular meetings here.

We also offer a bhakti-sastri diploma program which includes in-depth study of Bhagavad-gita, Nectar of Devotion, Nectar of Instruction and Sri Isopanisad.

Other activities include house programs, school programmes and weddings.
