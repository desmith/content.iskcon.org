Other Information:

Daily morning and evening programmes and Sunday Feasts.

Classes every Wednesday at 8:00 PM for harmonium and bhajan (devotional songs).

The temple also offers regular lectures on the primary Vedic texts.

Other programmes including Havans (fire sacrifices) can also be carried out at private homes. For further information call Nitai Dasa at 780-439-9999

Bookstore
The temple book store is the best place in Edmonton to acquire authorized books on the ancient science of Krishna Consciousness. Call 780-439-9999 for further information.
