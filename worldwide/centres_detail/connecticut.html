Brief details of centre
There has been a Hare Krishna Temple located in East Hartford Connecticut since 1978. We first rented a house on Silver Lane, and in February 1980 we purchased the present building. For the first few years there were several temple presidents, and in 1981 Pyari Mohan Prabhu came and has been Temple President ever since.

Deities: Sri Sri Jagannatha-Baladeva-Subadhra, Sri Sri Gaura Nitai

Other Information:

Daily morning programmes. Study sessions and feasts on Wednesday evenings and Sundays.

How to get there:

If you are south of East Hartford
Take I-91North to Exit 29 (I-84 East)
Get off first exit, Exit 90 (Route 5 North - Main Street)
At end of exit turn left
Go exactly two miles
The temple is the yellow house on the left.

If you are north of East Hartford:
Take I-91 South to Exit 35-A (291 East)
Get off at Exit 4 (Route 5)
At end of exit turn right
Go 1.8 miles and pass five lights
The temple is the yellow house on the right just before the sixth light.

If you are east of East Hartford:
Take I-84 West to Exit 61 (291 West)
Get off first exit, Exit 4 (Route 5)
At end of exit turn right
Go 1.9 miles and pass six lights
The temple is the yellow house on the right just before theseventh light.

If you are west of East Hartford:
Take Exit 56 (Governor St.) off I-84 (this exit is on the left)
At the end of the exit, turn right
At the first light, turn left and go pass three stop signs
At first light, turn left
The temple is the yellow house on the left just pass the next light.
