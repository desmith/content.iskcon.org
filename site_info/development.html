Development and Vision

This site is being developed in phases following a planning and consultation period of over a year. During this time we defined a vision for the website. The present site is the culmination of Phase One and is designed to cover some basic material and design ideas. New material is being prepared all the time and will be added to the site as it is produced. At the same time discussion and planning for Phase Two of the site is continuing.

Website goals
www.iskcon.com is being developed under the direction of Mukunda Maharaja, Anuttama Dasa, and Saunaka Rsi Dasa. We are also working in co-operation with BBT and BTG (www.krsna.com) to provide a comprehensive presentation of Krishna Consciousness on the internet.

The primary goals of iskcon.com are to:

  • Be the official website for ISKCON
  • Present a comprehensive coverage of all aspects of ISKCON
  • Present major establishments, projects, people and activities
  • Provide reference material for government, education, academia and the media
  • Provide authoritative information to interested parties, devotees and friends

Worldwide participation
If you would like to join the development team by donating time, skills or finances, please contact us at info@iskcon.com. Our team of volunteers works mainly through online communication so location is no obstacle.

Content
The current site is intended as a basis for a larger site. Here is a brief outline of our long-term plan.

Worldwide
To present each centre in a comprehensive manner, showing the Deities, the centre's history, facilities, and contact details. For larger centres we aim to provide iskcon.com as one of their main presences on the internet.

Culture
Highlighting Vaisnava culture, both historical and contemporary. A major aim of this section is to showcase the talents of devotees in all the arts.

Devotees
Presenting devotees, past and present, and their achievements.

Education
To provide educational raw materials as well as to provide online courses in co-operation with VTE (Vaisnava Training and Education). We are particularly searching for people with skills in online education.

News
ISKCON news taken from a range of news providers and also collected from submissions to the website

Community
Present ISKCON's role as a community and within the community. Highlight devotee projects.

Vaisnava youth
Engaging the younger generation in a meaningful way. For this section we are particularly interested in hearing from young devotees who would like to actively participate in design and content.

A home for devotee businesses and services
