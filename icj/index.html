ISKCON Communications Journal  
ISKCON Communications Journal (ICJ) is a journal of dialogue, focussing on issues related to missionary development in ISKCON (International Society for Krishna Consciousness) and with issues of communication, administration, social development and education which affect mission in ISKCON.

ICJ also provides a forum for members of various communities to comment on ISKCON's development.

This section of the website makes articles available which have appeared in previous issues of the Journal.

Articles can be found according to issue number, author, or title.

You can also find information about the Journal and its contributors, as well as subscription details. Articles will usually be put on-line six months after their publication date.
