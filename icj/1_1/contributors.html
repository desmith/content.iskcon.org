About the Contributors

Krsna Dharma Dasa is National Secretaryof UK Yatra. He joined ISKCON in 1979 and worked in a travellingsankirtan party until 1986 when he opened a centre in Manchester.He started Food for Life Manchester in 1989, which is currentlyserving approximately one hundred and fifty people daily from adaily run into the city and from a budget restaurant. He openedGovinda's Restaurant (with one hundred seat capacity) in the citycentre in 1992.
(Back to Article)

Satsvarupa Dasa Gosvami is oneof the first disciples of Srila Prabhupada. He is a prolific authorof Krsna conscious literature covering many aspects of spirituallife and including a six-volume biography of Srila Prabhupada.
(Back to Article)

Rasamandala Dasa joined ISKCONat Bhaktivedanta Manor in 1973 and was initiated by Srila Prabhupada.He has spent over eight years on book distribution, and has hada wide experience of lecturing and preaching throughout the UK.He is currently in charge of Communications in the UK and is Directorof ISKCON Educational Services, which oversees and promotes theSociety's interactions with schools and colleges.
(Back to Article)

Pranada Devi Dasi joined ISKCONin 1976. She received first initiation from Srila Prabhupada andsecond from Srila Satsvarupa Maharaja. She is the editor of Pritilaksanam, an internal paper discussing the many problems indevotee life.
(Back to Article)

Ravindra Svarupa Dasa is a discipleof Srila Prabhupada. He is author of many articles published inBack to Godhead magazine. He is a member of the BhaktivedantaInstitute, and ISKCON's International Governing Body Commission.
(Back to Article)
