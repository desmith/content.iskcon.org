Karatalas

Karatalas (manjira) are small Indian hand cymbals used at most ISKCON temples during devotional songs. They are used to emphasise the tal (or beat) of the songs. These cymbals are an easy musical instrument which all devotees can use. They involve banging the two cymbals in rhythmic fashion to the song.
