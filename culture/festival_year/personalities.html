Saints and Incarnations

Biographical information compiled by Krsna-priya Devi Dasi
(Vaisnava Academy for Girls, Alachua, Florida, USA)

Here is brief biographical information about the saints and incarnations whose appearance and disappearance days are listed by the Vaisnava calendar program. The calendar uses the term 'appearance' to denote the birth of these great souls and incarnations and the term 'disappearance' to denote their departure from this world.
