VISVARUPA was the elder brother of Lord Caitanya. He is considered a partial expansion of Lord Nityananda. At an early age he left home to take sannyasa. In 1431 he disappeared in Pandarpura in the district of Solapura, India.
(See Sri Caitanya- caritamrta, Adi-lila 10.106, 13.74-80, and 15.11-14 and Madhya- lila 9.299-300.)
