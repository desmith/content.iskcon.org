SITA THAKURANI was the wife of Sri Advaita Acarya. She was always absorbed in parental love for Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu. Because of Sita Devi's love, Saci Mata often chose her as the first person to worship Lord Caitanya during any auspicious ceremony.
(See Sri Caitanya-caritamrta, Adi-lila 13.111-118.)
