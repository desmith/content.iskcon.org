SIVANANDA SENA, an associate of Lord Caitanya, used all his possessions and wealth in the service of Lord Caitanya and His devotees. Every year he would lead a party of 200 devotees from Bengal to Jagannatha Puri to attend the annual Ratha-yatra festival. He would arrange for the devotees' food, tolls, ferries, and lodging. Sivananda Sena's family and servants were all dedicated to Lord Caitanya Mahaprabhu.
(See Sri Caitanya- caritamrta, Adi-lila 10.54-55 and 10.60-64, and Antya-lila 1.16- 32, 2.22-82, 10.142-151 and 12.15-53.)
